## What is Gamer Glossary?

Gamer Glossary is the definitive dictionary for gaming slang, terminology, and culture. Our mission is to catalog gaming language with the depth and context it deserves — not just definitions, but the stories, culture, and community behind each term.

## What We Cover

- **Core Slang** — The foundational terms every gamer should know
- **Genre-Specific Terms** — FPS callouts, MOBA mechanics, MMO raid terminology
- **Esports Culture** — Tournament terminology and competitive scene slang
- **Streaming & Content** — Twitch chat slang and content creator culture

## Our Methodology

Each term is researched using multiple sources: gaming forums, Reddit communities, esports broadcasts, game patch notes, and academic studies. We update entries regularly as gaming language evolves.
