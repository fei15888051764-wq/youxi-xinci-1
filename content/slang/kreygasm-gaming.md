Somebody hits a clip so filthy the chat moves like one organism. Ten thousand copies of the same face, scrolling too fast to read.

That face is Kreygasm, one of Twitch's original global emotes and the platform's oldest way of saying "that was disgusting (affectionate)."

## Why Kreygasm Hit Different

Why does chat still reach for this one? Because it's the connoisseur's reaction. Newer viewers spam whatever's trending this month. The old heads fire it when something is genuinely satisfying: a pixel-perfect combo, a speedrun PB, an absurd flick headshot.

In the channels I lurk in, it floods slower than the newer emotes but lands heavier. It's a nod from people who've been around since the Justin.tv days, and chat respects a veteran reaction face.

## When to Drop a Kreygasm

> "HE HIT THAT?? Kreygasm"
> "Kreygasm Kreygasm Kreygasm"
> "that combo was illegal in at least 40 states"

Classic. The Kreygasm meaning covers anything pleasurable: a nasty clip, a boss finally going down, a streamer's new setup reveal, even good food on a cooking stream. The name gives it a slightly NSFW flavor, but in practice it's just maximum satisfaction.

Timing matters, though. Drop it after the moment lands, not during the setup. Firing it early is the chat equivalent of laughing at a joke before the punchline.

The Melee community deserves a mention here. Fans decided years ago that PPMD's camera face after a big win was the human version of this emote, and honestly, the side-by-side comparisons hold up.

## The Origin of Kreygasm

Where did Kreygasm come from? The face belongs to Kreyg, a variety streamer who'd been broadcasting since the Justin.tv era, back in 2008. In March 2011 the site offered him a global emote. He says he turned it down at first.

Then his own chat coined the word on his stream, and he leaned in. He made the emote to fit the name, face and all. Twitch inherited it when the platform spun up, which makes it older than [PogChamp](/slang/pogchamp-gaming/)'s fame and most of the emote dictionary. Unlike [OMEGALUL](/slang/omegalul-gaming/), it never needed a third-party extension to blow up. It was global from day one.

## FAQ

**What does Kreygasm mean on Twitch?**

It's a global emote of the streamer Kreyg's face, and chat uses it to react to moments of pure satisfaction. Insane play, perfect timing, anything that feels good to watch. That's the whole job.

**Who's the face behind Kreygasm?**

Kreyg, a variety streamer from the Justin.tv era. The name started as a joke in his own chat, he embraced it, and the emote outlived the platform it was born on.

**Kreygasm vs PogChamp — when do you use which?**

Honestly, it comes down to flavor. [PogChamp](/slang/pogchamp-gaming/) is hype and disbelief: "I can't believe that happened." This one is pleasure: "that felt GOOD." Beautiful play, Kreygasm. Shocking play, PogChamp. If it's both, chat just spams everything.
