Touch grass is advice telling someone to go outside and take a break from gaming or the internet.

---

## Trajectory & Chronology

The phrase "touch grass" emerged from Twitter and Reddit around 2019 as a response to people who seemed overly invested in online arguments or gaming. It gained massive popularity during the 2020 pandemic when screen time skyrocketed. By 2022, it had become one of the most common pieces of advice in gaming communities, used both genuinely and sarcastically.

> **GEBILAOWANG:** Touch grass is the gaming community's way of looking out for each other — sometimes the best advice is to log off.

## Socio-Cultural Gain

"Touch grass" represents a growing awareness of gaming addiction and screen time issues. Unlike dismissive phrases like "get a life," touch grass is often used with genuine concern. It has spawned memes, merchandise, and even real-world events called "Touch Grass Meetups" where gamers socialize offline. The phrase has also entered mainstream vocabulary beyond gaming.
