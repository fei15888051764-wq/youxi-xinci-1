A boop is an environmental kill by pushing an opponent off the map, often used playfully. Originated from Overwatch but now used across multiple games.

---

## Trajectory & Chronology

The term "boop" originated from Overwatch in 2016, specifically from the character Lucio who says "Boop!" when using his Soundwave ability to push enemies off cliffs. The term quickly spread to other games with environmental hazards and became a beloved community meme. By 2018, "boop" was used across gaming to describe any playful push or environmental kill.

> **GEBILAOWANG:** The playful nature of "boop" makes it one of the most wholesome terms in gaming.

## Socio-Cultural Gain

Booping represents the lighter side of competitive gaming. Unlike toxic trash talk, a boop is usually accompanied by laughter and friendly banter. It has become a celebration of creative kills rather than just mechanical skill. The term also spawned countless memes and highlight reels dedicated to spectacular boop plays.

## FAQ

**Q1: Can only Lucio players say boop?**
**A:** No, the term is used across all games for any environmental push kill.

**Q2: Is booping considered toxic?**
**A:** Generally no, it's seen as playful and creative, unlike camping or spawn-killing.

**Q3: What's the best map for booping?**
**A:** Ilios Well and Lijiang Tower Night Market in Overwatch are boop havens.
