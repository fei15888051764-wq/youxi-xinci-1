C9 means abandoning the objective to chase kills, resulting in a preventable loss. Named after the esports team Cloud9 who famously made this mistake.

---

## Trajectory & Chronology

The term "C9" originated from the Overwatch League in 2018. The professional team Cloud9 (now London Spitfire) repeatedly abandoned the objective to chase kills, throwing away guaranteed wins. This happened so frequently that the community named the mistake after them. The term spread to all objective-based games by 2020.

> **GEBILAOWANG:** C9 is the perfect example of how one teams repeated mistake can become universal gaming vocabulary.

## Socio-Cultural Gain

C9ing represents the tension between individual glory and team objectives. Players want kills for their stats, but winning requires playing the objective. The term serves as a community warning against selfish play. It's also used humorously even in non-objective games to describe any preventable mistake caused by greed.
