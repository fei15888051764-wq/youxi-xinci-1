In gaming, 'BM' stands for 'Bad Manners' and describes rude, disrespectful, or unsportsmanlike behavior toward opponents or teammates. This includes taunting, excessive celebrating, intentionally delaying a won game, or any behavior that goes against the spirit of friendly competition.

---

## Trajectory & Chronology

The term 'BM' (Bad Manners) originated in early online multiplayer gaming communities in the late 1990s and early 2000s, particularly in StarCraft and fighting game communities where sportsmanship was highly valued. As competitive gaming grew, BM became a standard term for describing unsportsmanlike conduct. By the 2010s, 'BM' was universally understood across all competitive gaming genres. SmileLoverz documented in March 2026 that 'BM meaning slang remains actively used across platforms in 2026' and predicted that 'short behavioral labels like BM will stick around because humans always judge manners.'

## Socio-Cultural Gain

BM represents gaming culture's attempt to self-regulate sportsmanship. Unlike traditional sports with referees and formal codes of conduct, online gaming relies heavily on community norms. 'BM' is the vocabulary that enforces those norms. The concept of BM also reveals cultural differences in gaming. In Korean esports culture, BM is taken very seriously — excessive celebrating or taunting can damage a player's reputation and career. In Western gaming culture, BM is more tolerated, with many players viewing light taunting as 'part of the game.'

## FAQ

**Q1: What counts as BM?**

Common examples include: excessive taunting/emoting, delaying a won game, refusing to say GG, teabagging, and trash talking in all-chat.

**Q2: Is BM the same as being toxic?**

Not exactly. BM is usually about poor sportsmanship (taunting, disrespect) while toxicity is about harmful behavior (harassment, abuse).

**Q3: Can BM ever be acceptable?**

In some competitive contexts, light taunting is considered part of the mind games. But there's a line between competitive banter and genuine disrespect.

**Q4: How do I explain BM to a non-gamer in one sentence?**

"It's gaming slang for bad manners — being disrespectful to your opponents through taunting, gloating, or unsportsmanlike behavior."
